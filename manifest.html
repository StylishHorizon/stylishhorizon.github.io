Manifest

Your Memory Is Not a Cloud

Many people believe that old VHS or MiniDV tapes are a reliable archive. But manufacturers have said for a long time: their lifespan does not exceed 10–15 years. Over the years, the magnetic layer demagnetizes, crumbles, and the polymer base loses its properties. If these recordings are more than 20 years old, they are already living on "borrowed time." This is not just a tape; it is a family story. And this story can disappear forever.

We are here for those who understand that memory does not wait.

You Are Not a Courier. You Are a Guardian

In your hands is not just a tape, but a part of your life. Sending it on a journey across the country or the ocean means putting it at risk. Damage during shipping, customs errors, formalities... Why?

We believe your archive should always be under your control. That is why we do not force you to send it anywhere.

This Is Not for Everyone

We are not magicians, and we do not sell "lilac roses." We help those who value their archive but do not have the necessary knowledge or equipment.

If you understand why you need Adobe Premiere 2020, not 2025, to work with your video, you can do it yourself. If you know what IEEE-1384 is and can tell the difference between PCI and AGP—your path is DIY.

If you just want to save your memories without delving into technical details, we will find a trusted studio where you can go with your media and have the digitization done in your presence. You do not leave anything behind and do not have to worry about anything.

We Work With Your Voice

You decide what to keep and what to discard. Often, 3/4 of the video is just landscapes and long shots, but sometimes the most important moment is hidden in those very frames. We do not make decisions for you. We will transfer all the material to a file-sharing service, and you will choose what is important to you.

From Us - Only the Truth

We do not add "magic." We restore the video, clean up noise, and restore color, but we do not create artificial "nostalgia." The result is a clean, truthful video that is ready for further editing.

Your Format, Your Rules

Package 1: Legacy

  • For: Those who want to rescue aging footage and restore basic clarity.
  • What we do: We deinterlace, restore color, and remove digital noise.
  • The output: A standard video with a professional ProRes 422 codec, ready for editing.

Package 2: Ultimate Restoration

  • For: Clients seeking the highest fidelity for personal or professional use.
  • What we do: Everything in Legacy, plus: deeper color grading, sharpening, detail restoration, and frame-by-frame artifact removal.
  • The output: Video upscaled to Full HD (1080p) using the ProRes 422 codec.

Additional: UHD

  • For: The most demanding clients.
  • The output: Video upscaled to UHD (3840x2400).

We deliver the video to you in the format you prefer: on YouTube, a file-sharing service, in the cloud, or on a flash drive. We do not lock you into our services.
