Manifest

No hype. No noise. Just the facts on why your tapes are ticking—and how to stop the clock.

Your Memory Is Not a Cloud

Many think old VHS or MiniDV tapes last forever. Wrong. Manufacturers warn: 10-15 years max. The magnetic layer fades, crumbles; the plastic base warps. If yours are over 20 years old, they're on borrowed time. This isn't junk—it's your wedding, your kid's first steps. Gone forever if you wait.

Why risk it? We step in for those who get it: memory doesn't pause.

You Are Not a Courier. You Are a Guardian

That tape in your drawer? It's your life, not a package. Shipping it cross-country—or worse, overseas—invites disaster: bends, customs snags, lost in transit. Why gamble?

We scout the closest trusted studio for you—vetted by real rep, like social proof from followers. You drop off, watch it happen, walk out with control intact. No handoffs to strangers.

This Is Not for Everyone

We're no wizards peddling "lilac-tinted nostalgia." If you geek out on Adobe Premiere or spot the diff between PCI and AGP slots, grab your IEEE-1394 cable and DIY. You're set.

But if tech talk makes your eyes glaze—and you just want those memories safe—we handle it. No lectures, no upsells. We find the studio, you supervise. Simple.

We Work With Your Voice

You call the shots on cuts. Sure, 3/4 might be endless landscapes, but that "boring" pan could hide your big laugh. We don't play editor—we digitize it all, hand over full files via secure share.

You trim later, or not. We smooth scan lines (deinterlacing, no drama), balance colors naturally.

From Us - Only the Truth

No fairy dust. We scrub noise, fix colors in tools like Premiere—but skip fake "vintage glow." Result? Clean files ready for your edit, true to the original vibe.

Upscale? Only if you say so, via something like Topaz—no auto-magic. Your story, unaltered.

Your Format, Your Rules

ProRes for pros tweaking in post? Got it. VP9 for buttery YouTube playback? Yours. Or raw shares—no lock-in.

We match your setup, not ours.
