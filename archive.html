How It Flows

Upload raw files (unprocessed video from cassette) to a share link—studio scan or your own.

We deliver a free full preview: deinterlaced, on private YouTube. Comment timestamps there (e.g., "Keep full except 02:44-04:17"). Locked—can't edit or deny.

Approve? We finalize (people + tools for trim/upscale). 4-6 weeks. Pay after your ok on final length.
