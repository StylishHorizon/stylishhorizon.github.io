The Risk: Time Waits for No Tape

MiniDV, VHS, Hi8—the magnetic layers degrade in 10-15 years. Colors bleed, frames warp, audio hums into silence. Non-square pixels from PAL/NTSC distort proportions if untouched. Cloud "saves" mean copies, not cures—risking loss in hacks or obsolescence.

Why now? A wedding from 1998, first steps in 2002—they're not infinite. Delay, and echoes become ghosts.

Our Approach: Local Truth, No Illusion

No shipping across borders. We connect you to trusted local studios for capture—your tapes stay close, handled with care.

First pass: Deinterlace PAL/NTSC to PAR=1.0—no distortions for YouTube or archives. Gentle fixes (denoise, colors, square pixels, 50fps, audio normalize) without nostalgia filters. Stabilization only if shaky. Uploads expire in 14 days—secure, no eternal storage.

Packages: Legacy (720p ProRes, $5-15/min—for editors) or Ultimate (1080p upscale to 1920x1080/1200 for YT/Vimeo, $20-50/min). UHD 4K? On request—premium for the bold (prices? Let's say it's for cinephiles with deep pockets).

Process: Raw upload → free private YouTube preview (PAR-fixed) → comment trims → approve → 4-6 weeks finalize → pay only then. Full files in ProRes/VP9. Unfiltered clarity—your story, as it was.
